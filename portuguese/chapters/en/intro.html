INTRODUCTION

About

First, I'm not a linguist and I have no formal degree in the subject. I'm just an enthusiast with a general interest in languages.

This guide was first design to help a person that was struggling to find good Portuguese guides on the internet. But I just thought it would be a great idea to open to public and help the most people I can.

Target Audience

This is not Portuguese guide for beginners. It’s expected prior knowledge from the reader.

Unfortunately, I can’t recommend any specific guides for beginners, as I’m not familiar with any.

Format

I’ll try to keep the explanations as concise as possible while providing a good amount of practical examples, since I believe that examples and context are the best way to get a grasp on grammar and vocabulary.
