【とすれば1】

［Nとすれば］

（1）夫おっととすれば家事か じをおろそかにする妻つまには不満ふ まんも多おおいだろうと思おもう。／From the point of view of the husband, there’s probably a lot of dissatisfaction with a wife who neglects the housework.
（2）当事者とう じ しゃの彼かれとすれば、そう簡単かん たんに決きめるわけにはいかないのです。／As someone who is involved, it isn’t so easy for him to make a decision.
（3）教師きょう しとすれば、よくできる学生がく せいに関心かん しんが行いくのは自然し ぜんなことだと思おもう。／For teachers, I think it's natural to take more interest in students who perform well.
Placed after a noun denoting a person. Means “if I look at it/think about it from that person’s perspective.” More common in written language. In spoken language, the forms にしたら and/or にしてみれば are often used.
