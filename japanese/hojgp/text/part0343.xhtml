【それで】

（1）きのうの晩ばん熱ねつが出でて、それで今日きょうは学校がっ こうを休やすんだ。／I had a fever last night, so I didn’t go to school today.
（2）小ちいさい時ときに海うみでこわい思おもいをした。それで海うみが好すきになれない。／When I was little, I had a scary experience at the beach. That’s why I’ve never been able to like the ocean.
（3）A:来週らい しゅうから試験し けんだ。／Exams start next week.
B:それで。／So?
A:しばらく遊あそべない。／I can’t have fun for a while.
（4）A:昨日きのう、突然とつ ぜんいなかから親戚しん せきが出でてきまして...。／Yesterday one of my relatives arrived unexpectedly from my hometown (lit. the countryside)....
B:それで。／So?
A:それで、あのう今日きょうの残業ざん ぎょうは...。／So, um, about working overtime today....
B:かまわないよ、はやく帰かえりなさい。／That’s fine, just go home early.
Expresses a reason. May also be used to prompt the other person to continue speaking, as speaker B does in (3) and (4). Used in spoken language. A more informal expression is で.
