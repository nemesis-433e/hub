【せめて】

1. せめて

（1）夏なつはせめて一週間いっ しゅう かんぐらい休やすみがほしい。／I’d like at least one week of vacation in the summer.
（2）大学だい がくに入はいったのだから、せめて教員きょう いん免許めんきょぐらい取とっておこうと思おもう。／Now that I’m in college, I think I should at least get a teaching certificate.
（3）小ちいさくてもいい。せめて庭にわのある家いえに住すみたい。／I don’t mind if it’s tiny. I want to live in a house that at least has a garden.
（4）せめてあと三日みっ かあれば、もうちょっといい作品さく ひんが出だせるのだが。／If only I had three more days, I’d be able to produce a slightly better-quality piece.
（5）あしたが無理む りなら、せめてあさってぐらいまでに金かねを返かえしてほしい。／If you can’t do it tomorrow, I’d like you to return the money by at least the day after.
Means “it’s not enough but at least that much.” Followed by expressions of intention or wish. Often used in the pattern せめて...ぐらいは. Example (1) means “while a long vacation isn’t possible, I would like to have at least one week.”

2. せめて...だけでも

（1）せめて一晩ひと ばんだけでも泊とめてもらえませんか。／Couldn’t you let me stay at least one night?
（2）忙いそがしいのはわかっているけど、せめて日曜日にち よう びだけでも子供こ どもと遊あそんでやってよ。／I know you’re busy, but play with the kids at least on Sundays, OK?
（3）うちは子供こ どもに継つがせるような財産ざい さんはなにもないので、せめて教育きょう いくだけでもと思おもって無理む りをして大学だい がくへやっているのです。／We don’t have any assets to pass on to our children, but we thought we should at least give them an education, so even though we can’t afford it, we’re sending them to college.
（4）両親りょう しんを早はやくなくして、苦労く ろうしました。せめて母親はは おやだけでも生いきていてくれたらと思おもいます。／Life was hard after losing my parents when I was so young. I really wish at least my mother were still alive.
Means “it’s not enough but at least that much.” (1) above means “even just one night would be all right.”

3. せめて...なりとも

（1）せめて一目ひと めなりとも子供こ どもに会あいたいものだ。／I wish I could see my children, even just for a brief moment.
（2）せめて一晩ひと ばんなりとも部屋へ やを貸かしてはいただけないでしょうか。／Wouldn’t it be possible for you to let me have a room for just one night?
Means the same as せめて...だけでも. A literary expression.

4. せめてものN

（1）ひどい事故じ こだったが、死者し しゃが出でなかったのがせめてもの救すくいだ。／It was a terrible accident, but at least we can take comfort in the fact that there were no casualties.
（2）パスポートをとられなかったのが、せめてものなぐさめだ。／It’s a relief that at least my passport wasn’t stolen.
（3）せめてものお礼れいのしるしにこれを受うけ取とってください。／Please take this as a small token of my appreciation.
Means that, in comparison to a potentially worse situation, it’s a good thing this is all that happened. The choice of nouns that can be used in the N slot is limited to 救い(help/salvation), なぐさめ(comfort/consolation), etc. Example (3) is an idiomatic expression that means “it’s not sufficient, but it’s a sign of my appreciation.”
