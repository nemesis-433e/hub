【もうすぐ】

（1）田中た なかさんはもうすぐ来きます。／Tanaka-san will arrive soon.
（2）もうすぐ夏休なつ やすみですね。／It’s almost summer vacation!
（3）クリスマスまで、もうすぐだ。／It’s not long until Christmas.
（4）桜さくらの花はなももうすぐ咲さきそうだ。／The cherry blossoms also will soon be blooming.
（5）もうすぐここに30階建かい だてのマンションが建たつそうだ。／I hear that a thirty-story apartment building will soon be built here.
Expresses that there is not much time from the present until a certain thing occurs. Refers to a longer period of time than that referred to by すぐ. Often used in spoken language.
