【さい】

［Nのさい（に）］

［Vさい（に）］

（1）お降おりのさいは、お忘わすれ物もののないよう、お気きをつけください。／When getting off (the train), be sure not to leave anything behind.
（2）先日せん じつ京都きょう とへ行いった際さい、小学校しょう がっ こうのときの同級生どう きゅう せいをたずねた。／When I went to Kyoto the other day, I visited a classmate of mine from elementary school.
（3）このさい、おもいきって家族か ぞくみんなでスペインにひっこさない？／This is an opportunity, so why don’t we take the plunge and move to Spain all together as a family?
（4）国際こくさい会議かい ぎを本県ほん けんで開催かい さいされる際さいには、次回じ かいはぜひとも我わが市しの施設し せつをお使つかいくださるよう、市長し ちょうとしてお願ねがい申もうし上あげます。／When an international conference is held in this prefecture, I as mayor of this city beg that next time you use our city’s facilities.
Can often be paraphrased as とき. Differences between さい and とき include: (a) さい is more formal than とき, (b) さい has the additional meaning of opportunity, chance, or trigger, and (c) さい is less likely to be used with negative forms than とき. Also, it should be noted that このさい, as in (3), is an idiomatic expression used when a decision is made by resolutely seizing an opportunity, and as such it cannot be paraphrased as とき.
