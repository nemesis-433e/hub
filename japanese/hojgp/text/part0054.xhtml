【いちど】

1. いちど　Vと／V-たら

（1）タイ料理りょう りは一度いち ど食たべると病やみつきになる。／Once you have tasted Thai food, you will get addicted to it.
（2）あの森もりは一度いち ど迷まよい込こんだらなかなか外そとに出でられないらしい。／I heard that once you get lost in that forest, you can’t find your way out of it easily.
（3）あの作家さっ かの小説しょう せつは一度いち ど読よみ始はじめるとついつい最後さい ごまで一気いっ きに読よんでしまう。／Once I start to read a book by that novelist, I read to the very end in a single sitting.
（4）一度いち どいいワインの味あじを知しってしまうと、もう安物やす ものは飲のめなくなる。／Once you get to know what a good wine tastes like, you can’t drink a cheap one anymore.
Means “once a person experiences something/is in a certain state, s/he cannot go back to the previous state.”

2. いちど　V-ば／V-たら

（1）こんなところは一度いち ど来くればたくさんだ。／It’s enough to come to a place like this only once.
（2）一度いち どこういう苦労く ろうを経験けい けんしておけばもう安心あん しんだ。何なにがあっても耐たえられる。／Once I have experienced such a hardship, I’ll have no worries. I can overcome whatever happens.
（3）一度いち どやり方かたがわかれば、後あとは応用おう ようがきく。／Once you understand how to do it, then you can apply it.
Means “once I experience/understand something, that is sufficient/even if something similar happens, things will somehow turn out.” Implies “I don’t need to do it again.” When followed by the description of a continuing state, as in (3), it can be replaced by いちどVと.
