【それなら】

（1）A:どこか山登やま のぼりに行いこうと思おもうんだけど。／I’m thinking of going mountain-climbing somewhere.
B:それなら、日本に ほんアルプスがいいよ。／Then the Japanese alps would be ideal.
（2）A:パーティーにはリーさんの奥おくさんも来くるそうだ。／Apparently Lee-san wife will also attend the party.
B:それなら私わたしも行いきたいわ。／Then I’d like to go, too.
（3）これ以上い じょうの援助えん じょはできないといっているが、それならこちらにも考かんがえがある。／They’re saying they can’t offer any more assistance. Well, if that’s the case, then we have some ideas of our own about this.
Used in response to something the other person has said and means “if that is so, in that case.”
