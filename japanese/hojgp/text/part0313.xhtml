【せつな】

［V-たせつな］

（1）目めを離はなしたせつな、子供こ どもは波なみにのまれていった。／The moment I glanced away, the child was swept off by a wave.
（2）あたり一面いち めん火ひの海うみだった。逃にげてきた道みちをふりかえったそのせつな、建物たて ものが轟音ごう おんをたててくずれおちた。／There was a sea of fire all around me. The moment I looked back at the road I’d escaped along, the building collapsed with a thunderous roar.
Means “a short time, an instant.” Its range of use is narrower than that of 瞬間, and it has a strong literary flavor. Tends to be used in written language.
