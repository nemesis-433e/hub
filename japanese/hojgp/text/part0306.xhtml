【すむ】

1. ...すむ

［Nですむ］

［V-てすむ］

（1）もっと費用ひ ようがかかると思おもったが2万まん円えんですんだ。／I thought it would cost more, but it only took 20,000 yen.
（2）用事よう じは電話でん わですんだ。／I managed to take care of it over the phone.
（3）金かねですむなら、いくらでも出だします。／If it’s only a matter of money, I’ll pay as much as necessary.
（4）ガラスを割わってしまったが、あやまっただけで済すんだ。／I broke the glass, but managed to get away with only an apology.
（5）あやまってすむこととすまないことがある。／There are things you can settle with an apology and things you can’t.
The original meaning of すむ is “end” or “finish,” but it is used to mean “that’s all I needed to do” or “fortunately, it wasn’t necessary to do anything more complicated.”

2. V-ないで／V-ずに　すむ

（1）バスがすぐに来きたので待またないですんだ。／The bus came right away, so I didn’t have to wait.
（2）バスがすぐに来きたので待またずにすんだ。／The bus came right away, so I didn’t have to wait.
（3）電話でん わで話はなしがついたので行いかずにすんだ。／I managed to take care of it over the phone, so I didn’t have to go.
（4）古ふるい自転車じ てん しゃをもらったので、買かわないで済すんだ。／I was given an old bicycle, so I didn’t have to buy one.
Means “I no longer need to do what I had planned to do” or “I can avoid something I had expected.” Expresses that an undesirable situation can be avoided.

3. ...すむことではない

［Nですむことではない］

［V-てすむことではない］

（1）大事だい じな書類しょ るいをなくしてしまうなんて、謝あやまってすむことではない。／Losing an important document isn’t something you can get away with just by apologizing.
（2）少数意見しょう すう い けんだと片付かた づけてすむことではない。／You can’t dismiss it as being a minority opinion.
（3）この問題もん だいは補償金ほ しょう きんで済すむことではない。心こころからの謝罪しゃ ざいが必要ひつ ようだ。／This is not an issue that can be resolved by financial compensation. A wholehearted apology is necessary.
Means “you can’t deal with an issue by doing such-and-such; simply doing so would not be sufficient.” In (1), it is used to mean “an apology won’t make up for it; it can’t be undone,” and in (2) it means “you can’t ignore it just because it’s a minority opinion.”
