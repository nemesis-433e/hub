【ところに】

［V-ている／V-た ところに］

（1）出掛で かけようとしたところに電話でん わがかかってきた。／Just as we were leaving, the telephone rang.
（2）ようやく実行じっ こうする方向ほう こうに意見い けんがまとまったところへ思おもわぬ邪魔じゃ まが入はいった。／We had finally agreed on a plan of action when there was an unexpected snag.
（3）財布さい ふをなくして困こまっているところに偶然ぐう ぜん知しり合あいが通とおりかかり、無事ぶ じ家いえまでたどり着つくことができた。／When I was in a real fix after losing my wallet, an acquaintance happened to come by and I was able to get home safely.
Takes the form ...ところに or ...ところへ and means “when I was ...ing” or “when I did....”
Used to express the occurrence of an event that alters or changes a situation that is at a certain stage. In most cases, the event is one that impedes or blocks something’s progress, as in (1) and (2). However, it can also be something that changes the current situation for the better, as in (3).
