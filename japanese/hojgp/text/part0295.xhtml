【ずつ】

［数量詞+ずつ］

（1）一人ひとりに3つずつキャンディーをあげましょう。／I’ll give each of you three pieces of candy.
（2）5人にんずつでグループを作つくった。／We made groups of five.
（3）雪ゆきが溶とけて、少すこしずつ春はるが近ちかづいてくる。／The snow is melting, and spring is gradually approaching.
（4）いくらかずつでもお金かねを出だし合あって、焼やけた寺てらの再建さい けんに協力きょう りょくしよう。／Let’s each give whatever small amount of money we can and work together to rebuild the temple that got burned down.
（5）病人びょう にんはわずかずつだが食たべられるようになってきた。／The patient has started to be able to eat small amounts of food.
Means “the same amount to each” or “repeat approximately the same amount.” Example (1) means “three pieces each,” (2) means “five people in one group,” and (5) means “a little each time.”
