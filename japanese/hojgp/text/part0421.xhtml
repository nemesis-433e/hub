【つ...つ】

［R-つR-つ］

（1）彼かれに会あおうか会あうまいかと悩なやんで、家いえの前まえを行いきつ戻もどりつしていた。／I kept walking to and fro in front of his house, worrying over whether or not I should meet him.
（2）お互たがい持もちつ持もたれつで、助たすけ合あいましょう。／Let’s make it a give-and-take as we help each other.
（3）初詣はつ もうでの神社じん じゃはものすごい人出ひと でで、押おしつ押おされつ、やっとのことで境内けい だいまでたどり着ついた。／The shrine for the first pilgrimage of the New Year was packed with people, and after pushing and being pushed, we finally made it into the inner grounds.
（4）久ひさしぶりに友人ゆう じんとさしつさされつ酒さけを飲のんで何時間なん じ かんもしゃべった。／For the first time in quite a while, I talked with a friend for many hours while we filled each other’s cup with sake.
Expresses that two actions alternate, by repeating verbs with opposite meaning (e.g. 行く(go)- 戻る(return)) or active and passive verbs (e.g. 押す(push)- 押される(being pushed)). Used in set expressions, such as 行きつ戻りつ or 持ちつ持たれつ, as in (1) and (3) respectively.
