【みこんで】

［...をみこんで］

［Nをみこんで］

［V-るのをみこんで］

（1）君きみを見込み こんで頼たのむのだが、ぜひ今度こん どの仕事し ごとに参加さん かしてほしい。／I make this request with full confidence in you and certainly hope that you will participate in future projects.
（2）君きみを男おとこと見込み こんで頼たのみたいことがある。／I have something I want to ask you as a man of the world.
（3）完成かん せいまでに時間じ かんがかかる地下鉄ち か てつ工事こう じなどは、物価ぶっ かの上昇じょう しょうを見込み こんで、余裕よ ゆうのある予算よ さんを組くんでおいた方ほうがよい。／In anticipation of rising commodity prices, it is better if projects which take time to complete, such as subway construction, have some surplus in the budget.
（4）商品しょう ひんには、はじめから売うれ残のこるのを見込み こんだ値段ね だんがつけてある。／From the start, the price of commodities includes anticipated surplus stocks.
Means “anticipating...” or “expecting....” As in examples (1) and (2), it demonstrates that, a person’s knowledge being valued highly, there is the expectation that s/he will do an excellent job. Also, as in (3) and (4), it expresses “(something) being accounted for from the beginning,” and “expectations being formed in advance.”
