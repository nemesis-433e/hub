【そのうえ】

（1）あそこのレストランは高たかくて、そのうえまずい。／That restaurant is expensive, and what is more, the food is awful.
（2）彼かれの奥おくさんは美人び じんだし、そのうえ料理りょう りもうまい。／His wife is a real beauty and—on top of that—is a good cook, too.
（3）あの人ひとにはすっかりお世話せ わになった。住すむところから、役所やく しょの手続て つづきまで。そのうえアルバイトまで紹介しょう かいしてもらった。／I’m really indebted to that person. For everything from finding me a place to live to helping me with the procedures at government offices. And on top of that, he also found me a part-time job.
（4）きのうは先生せん せいの家いえでごちそうになった。帰かえりにはおみやげまでもらい、そのうえ車くるまで駅えきまで送おくっていただいた。／Yesterday my teacher treated me to a meal at her house. Before I went home, she even gave me a gift, and in addition to all that, she gave me a ride to the train station too.
An expression used to add something similar. Used to add information about situations or circumstances, in order to describe them in more detail. Can be replaced by それに.
