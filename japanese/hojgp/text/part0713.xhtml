【なり1】

1. V-るなり

（1）家いえに帰かえるなり自分じ ぶんの部屋へ やに閉とじ込こもって出でてこない。／As soon as he came home, he locked himself up in his room.
（2）立たち上あがるなり目めまいがして倒たおれそうになった。／When I stood up, I suddenly felt dizzy and was about to collapse.
（3）会あうなり金かねを貸かしてくれなどと言いうので驚おどろいた。／I was very surprised because as soon as she saw me, she asked me to lend her money and more.
With an action verb, V-るなり expresses the meaning of “right after that action.” This expression is similar to ...したとたんに or ...するやいなや. V-るなり is used when something unexpected happens right after the action.

2. V-たなり

a. V-たなり（で）

（1）座すわったなり動うごこうともしない。／Since he sat down, he has not moved at all.
（2）うつむいたなり黙だまりこんでいる。／She has been silent since she bowed her head.
（3）立たったなりでじっとこちらの様子よう すを伺うかがっている。／He’s been standing there and watching our every action.
Expresses a state which continues as it is and does not get anything/circumstances moving. Can be rephrased as ...したまま. V-たなり sounds a little archaic.

b. V-たなり

（1）家いえを出でたなり一いっカ月げつも帰かえってこなかった。／Since he left home, a month has passed.
（2）お辞儀じ ぎをしたなり何なにも言いわずに部屋へ やを出でて行いった。／As soon as she bowed, she left the room without saying a word.
（3）住民じゅう みんの反対はん たいにあって、工事こう じは中断ちゅう だんされたなり解決かい けつのめどもついていない。／Because of the objection of the residents, the construction was interrupted and there is no sign of any solution when the problem will be solved.
Expresses a state which continues to be as it is without changing even though it is normally expected to trigger the following state after something happens. Can be rephrased as ...したまま. Sounds a little archaic.
