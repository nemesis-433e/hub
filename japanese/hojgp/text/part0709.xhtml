【ならいい】

［N／Na　ならいい］

［A／V　（の）ならいい］

（1）お母かあさんが病気びょう きならいいよ。早はやく家いえに帰かえってあげなさい。／If your mother is unwell, don’t worry (about this). Go home and take care of her.
（2）勉強べん きょうがそんなに嫌きらいならいいよ。大学だい がくなど行いかないで就職しゅう しょくしたらいい。／If you hate studying that much, I won’t force you. You should get a job rather than something like going to a university.
（3）A:悪わるいけど、その仕事し ごとはあまり得意とく いじゃないんだ。／I’m sorry, but I’m not very good at that kind of job.
B:やりたくない（の）ならいいよ。他ほかの人ひとに頼たのむから。／If you don’t want to do it, that’s all right. I’ll ask somebody else.
（4）それほど熱心ねっ しんに言いう（の）ならいいじゃありませんか。やりたいことをやらせてあげなさいよ。／Isn’t it all right if she says she’s that enthusiastic? Go ahead and let her do what she wants.
On the basis of the previous utterance or situation, ならいい expresses the speaker’s attitudes such as allowance or non-intervention and means “considering the previous utterance or situation, it’s okay to do something/you can do it/you don’t have to do it.” For instance, the sentence in (3) means “if you don’t want to do that, you don’t have to do it.”
