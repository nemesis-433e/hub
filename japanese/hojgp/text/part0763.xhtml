【にかまけて】

［Nにかまけて］

（1）仕事し ごとにかまけてちっとも子供こ どもの相手あい てをしてやらない。／She is too busy at her work to take care of her child.
（2）遊あそびにかまけて勉強べん きょうしようともしない。／He is too busy playing to try to study.
（3）資料し りょうの整理せい りにばかりかまけていては、仕事し ごとは前まえへ進すすまない。／I am very busy just sorting the materials, so my work doesn’t progress.
With a noun expressing a thing/state/event, expresses the meaning that someone devotes his/her energies to something without looking at other things. Often followed by some negative expression meaning that the other things are disregarded and neglected.
