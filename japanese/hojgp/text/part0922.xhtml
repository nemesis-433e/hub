【べつだん】

1. べつだん...ない

（1）べつだん変かわったことはない。／Nothing has really changed.
（2）彼かれはいつもより口数くち かずが少すくないようだったが、私わたしはべつだん気きにもしなかった。／He seemed less talkative than usual, but I did not pay any attention to it.
Means “not especially” or “nothing particularly.” It is a slightly formal expression with the tone of written language.

2. べつだんのN

（1）別段べつ だんのご配慮はい りょをいただきたく存ぞんじます。／We would be grateful for your special attention to this matter.
（2）来賓らい ひんとして招まねかれて、別段べつ だんの扱あつかいを受うけた。／Being invited as a guest, I was treated exceptionally well.
Means “special” or “extraordinary.” (1) is a very formal expression.
