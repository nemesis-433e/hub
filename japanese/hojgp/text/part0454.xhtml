【てください】

［V-てください］

（1）今週中こん しゅう ちゅうに履歴書り れき しょを出だしてください。／Please submit your CV by the end of this week.
（2）来週らい しゅうまでにこの本ほんを読よんでおいてくださいね。／Please read this book by the end of next week, OK?
（3）この薬くすりは1日にち3回かい、毎食後まい しょく ごに飲のんでください。／Please take this medicine after each meal three times a day.
（4）授業じゅ ぎょうはできるだけ遅刻ち こくしないでください。／Please make every effort not to be late for class.
（5）頼たのむから、邪魔じゃ ましないでくださいよ。／I beg you not to disturb me.
Expresses requests or orders that something be done for the benefit of the speaker (or someone associated with the speaker).
This is more formal than V-てくれ but is only used when it is natural or expected for the listener to do the action. Used when addressing someone equal or junior in position or age.
