【にもならない】

1. Nにもならない

（1）あまりにばかばかしい話はなしで、冗談じょう だんにもならない。／This is such a silly story that I can’t accept it even as a joke.
（2）こんなに細ほそい木きでは焚たきつけにもならない。／A piece of wood this thin can’t even be used as kindling.
Follows a noun that denotes something of limited usefulness, such as 冗談 (joke) or 焚きつけ (kindling), and means that the thing in question does not have even that much value.

2. V-るきにもならない

（1）あまりにばかばかしくて笑わらう気きにもならない。／It’s too ridiculous even to make me feel like laughing.
（2）彼かれの考かんがえ方かたがあまりに子供こ どもっぽいので、腹はらを立たてる気きにもならなかった。／His way of thinking was so childish that I couldn’t even get angry.
Means “I’m not getting the feeling that I want to do such-and-such.” In many cases, implies a negative evaluation on the part of the speaker, in the sense that the thing referred to in the first clause has such little value that it doesn’t make him/her feel like doing the action in question.
