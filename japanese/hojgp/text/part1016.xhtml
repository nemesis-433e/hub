【やたらに】

（1）今日きょうはやたらに忙いそがしい一日いち にちだった。／Today was an extremely busy day.
（2）最近さい きんやたらにのどがかわく。なにか病気びょう きかもしれない。／Recently, I have been excessively thirsty. Perhaps I have some illness.
（3）今年ことしの夏なつはやたらに雨あめが多おおい。／This summer there has been an extremely large amount of rain.
（4）彼かれは、女子じょ し学生がく せいとみると、やたらに話はなしかけては嫌きらわれているようだ。／It seems that he has been making himself unpopular by speaking to every female student when he happens to see one.
（5）この学校がっ こうはやたらに規則き そくを変更へん こうするので困こまる。／It is a nuisance that this school changes the rules without considering the repercussions.
Expresses that the extent of something is extreme or that the situation is irregular. やたらと is also used. There are also the expressions むやみやたらに and めったやったらに.
