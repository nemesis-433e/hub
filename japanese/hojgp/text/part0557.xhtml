【とおして】

1. Nをとおして〈intermediary〉

（1）私わたしたちは友人ゆう じんを通とおして知しり合あいになった。／We got to know each other through a mutual friend.
（2）我々われ われは体験たい けんばかりでなく書物しょ もつを通とおして様々さま ざまな知識ち しきを得えることができる。／We can gain a range of knowledge not only from experience but also through books.
（3）実験じっ けんを通とおして得えられた結果けっ かしか信用しん ようできない。／I only trust results obtained through experiments.
（4）5年間ねん かんの文通ぶん つうを通とおして二人ふたりは恋こいを実みのらせた。／Through five years of correspondence, the two grew to love each other.
（5）今日こん にちでは、マスメディアを通とおして、その日ひのうちに世界せ かいの出来事で き ごとを知しることができる。／Nowadays, through the mass media, we can find out about world events on the same day they happen.
Follows a noun that represents a person, thing, or action and indicates its use as an agent or means by which something is achieved. Used to say that knowledge or experience is gained through the noun in question.

2. V-ることをとおして

（1）子供こ どもは学校がっ こうで他ほかの子供こ どもと一緒いっ しょに遊あそんだり学まなんだりすることを通とおして社会生活しゃ かい せい かつのルールを学まなんで行いく。／Children learn social norms for living through playing and learning with other children at school.
（2）教師きょう しは学生がく せいに教おしえることを通とおして、逆ぎゃくに学生がく せいから教おしえられることも多おおい。／Through teaching, it is conversely the educators who also learn much from their students.
Follows the dictionary form of a verb and expresses the same meaning as 1 above. Typically combines with verbs of Japanese origin, such as 学ぶ (learn/study). When combined with verbs of Chinese origin, such as 学習する (study/learn) or 研究する (research), usually the pattern shown in Usage 1 is used, as in 学習をとおして (through learning) and 研究をとおして (through research).

3. Nをとおして〈during a period of time〉

（1）5日か間かんを通とおしての会議かい ぎで、様々さま ざまな意見い けんが交換こう かんされた。／Over the five days of the conference, a wide range of opinions were exchanged.
（2）この地方ち ほうは1年ねんを通とおして雨あめの降ふる日ひが少すくない。／This region has few rainy days throughout the year.
（3）この1週間しゅう かんを通とおして、外そとに出でたのはたったの2度どだけだ。／During this whole week, I have stepped outside only twice.
Used after expressions that indicate a period of time and means “during that period” or “within that time frame.” May express an action that continues uninterrupted throughout the time period, as in (1), or the intermittent occurrence of an event during a set period, as in (2) and (3).
