【やむ】

［R-やむ］

（1）夜中よ なかの三時さん じごろになってやっと赤あかん坊ぼうは泣なきやんだ。／At around three in the morning the baby at last stopped crying.
（2）となりの部屋へ やの電話でん わのベルが鳴なりやんだ。／The telephone in the next room stopped ringing.
（3）一ヶ月いっ か げつ降ふり続つづいた雨あめが降ふりやんだ後あとは一面いち めんの洪水こう ずいだった。／After the rain which had been falling for one month stopped, the whole area was flooded.
Means “a phenomenon, which had been continuing, stops.” Used together with limited intransitive verbs such as 泣く(cry), 鳴る(sound) and 降る(raining/snowing). 降りやむ(stop raining/snowing) is usually abbreviated to やむ.
