【はじめて】

［V-て（みて）はじめて］

（1）病気びょう きになってはじめて健康けん こうのありがたさがわかる。／People don’t know the value of good health until they become ill.
（2）外国がい こくに行いって初はじめて自分じ ぶんの国くにについて何なにも知しらないことに気きがついた。／I first realized that I knew nothing about my own country only when I went abroad.
（3）言いわれてみて初はじめて、自分じ ぶんがいかに狭量きょう りょうであったかに気きがついた。／I didn’t realize how narrow-minded I was until someone told me so.
Means “realize for the first time only after something happens.” Used to express that, as the result of a certain experience, a person has realized something that s/he didn’t previously know, or something that s/he did know but had never thought about very seriously.
