【ぶん】

1. ...ぶん

［Nのぶん］

［Vぶん］

［期間を表す名詞+ぶん］

（1）甘あまいものが大好だい すきな弟おとうとは、私わたしのぶんのケーキまで食たべてしまった。／My brother, who has a sweet tooth, has eaten my share of the cake.
（2）心配しん ぱいしなくていいよ。君きみのぶんはちゃんと残のこしておいたから。／You don’t need to worry. I have put aside your share.
（3）子供こ どもに食たべさせる分ぶんまで奪うばわれてしまった。／Even the portion kept for the children has been taken.
（4）来月分らい げつ ぶんの食費しょく ひまで先さきに使つかってしまった。／I have spent next month’s food allowance already.
（5）部屋へ やを借かりるためには、はじめに家賃や ちん三さんヶ月分か げつ ぶんのお金かねが必要ひつ ようです。／In order to rent an apartment, three months’ rent as a down payment is required up front.
Means a portion, allocation, or something for a specific purpose. (4) means that “the money put aside for next month’s food has been spent already,” and (5) means “the amount of money equivalent to three months’ rent.”

2. ...ぶん（だけ）

［Nのぶん］

［Naなぶん］

［A／V　ぶん］

（1）1年間ねん かんの休職きゅう しょくの分ぶんだけ、仕事し ごとがたまっていた。／A year’s worth of work piled up during my 12-month absence.
（2）外そとで元気げん きな分ぶん、彼かれは家いえではおとなしい。／He is as quiet at home as he is outgoing in public.
（3）食たべれば食たべたぶん（だけ）太ふとる。／I put on as much weight as I eat.
（4）早はやく始はじめれば、その分ぶん（だけ）仕事し ごとが早はやく終おわる。／If we start early, we can finish the work that much quicker.
（5）彼かれを信頼しん らいしていたぶん（だけ）裏切うら ぎられたときのショックも大おおきかった。／My disappointment matched the trust I had placed in him.
Means that the level matches the thing that it is being compared to.
As in the cases of (3) and (4), it is often used in the pattern ...V-ばV-たぶんだけ or ...V-ば、そのぶんだけ. It indicates “that much” or “the equivalent amount.”
(3) means that one puts on as much weight as one eats. (4) means that the work gets done that much quicker if it is started earlier. (5) means that the high level of trust placed in him is proportionate to the degree of shock felt. だけ may be abbreviated.

3. このぶんでいくと

このぶんでは

（1）一年いち ねんかかって、まだ半分はん ぶんも終おわっていない。このぶんでいくと完成かん せいするには三年さん ねんぐらいかかりそうだ。／It has taken a year and we haven’t done even half of what we need to do. At this rate, it will take three years or so before we complete it.
（2）このぶんでは徹夜てつ やになりそうだ。／The way things are going, we will be working through the night.
（3）このぶんでいくと、仕事し ごとは予定よ ていより早はやく終おわりそうだ。／If everything goes on like this, the work will finish sooner than planned.
Means “if it goes on like this” or “if it continues at this pace.”

4. ...ぶんには

［Naなぶんには］

［A／V　ぶんには］

（1）はたで見みているぶんには楽らくそうだが、自分じ ぶんでやってみるとどんなに大変たい へんかがわかる。／It looks like fun if you are an onlooker, but if you try it yourself, you will know how hard it is.
（2）私わたしはいかなる宗教しゅう きょうも信しんじない。しかし、他人た にんが信しんじるぶんには一向いっ こうにかまわない。／I do not believe in any religion. However, I do not object in any way to other people’s beliefs.
（3）A:申もうし訳わけありません。会議かい ぎの始はじまる時間じ かんがいつもより少すこし遅おそくなりそうなんですが。／I am sorry to inform you that the meeting is likely to start a little later than usual.
B:遅おそくなるぶんには、かまわないよ。／I wouldn’t mind the meeting starting a little later (rather than starting earlier).
Means “as long as.” (1) means “as long as one does not participate but only observes, it looks like fun.” (2) means, “I don’t believe in any religion, but I do not mind if other people choose to believe in one.” (3) means, “starting the meeting later is acceptable, but starting it sooner is not.”
