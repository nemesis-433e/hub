【まわる】

［R-まわる］

（1）この寒さむいのに子供達こ ども たちは外そとを走はしり回まわっている。／Even though it is this cold, the children are running around outside.
（2）病人びょう にんがスイカが食たべたいというので、スイカを求もとめて 12 月がつの街まちを駈かけずり回まわった。／Because the sick person says that he wants to eat watermelon, I have been dashing around the December streets searching for watermelon.
（3）売うれっ子こジャーナリストの彼かれは世界中せ かい じゅうを飛とび回まわっている。／He is a popular journalist who flies all over the world.
（4）子供こ どもは小犬こ いぬに追おいかけられて、部屋中へ や じゅうを逃にげまわった。／Chased by the puppy, the child ran all around the room to get away.
Used together with verbs which express movement like 動く (move), 走る (run), 飛ぶ (fly), 泳ぐ (swim), and verbs which express activity such as 暴れる (rage), 遊ぶ (play), and 跳ねる (jump), this means “to do ... here and there” or “to do ... in that area.”
