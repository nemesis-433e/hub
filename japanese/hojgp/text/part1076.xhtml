【をつうじて】

1. NをつうじてV

（1）その話はなしは山田やま ださんを通つうじて相手あい てにもつたわっているはずです。／I expect that Yamada-san has told that story to the other party, too.
（2）A社しゃはB社しゃを通つうじてC社しゃとも提携ていけい関係かん けいにある。／Company A has a joint venture with company C, through company B.
（3）現地げん ちの大使館たい し かんを通つうじて外務省がい む しょうにはいった情報じょう ほうによると、死者し しゃは少すくなくとも 100 人にんをこえたもようである。／According to the information received by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs from the local embassy the number of casualties has exceeded at least 100 people.
Means “via....” This is used to detail that information is conveyed, relationships are built etc. via something. The thing which is conveyed is information, stories, messages etc. but cannot be used in reference to means of transportation.
（×）この列車はマドリッドをつうじてパリまで行く。／This train is going to Paris, via Madrid.
（○）この列車はマドリッド｛を通って／を経由して｝パリまで行く。／This train is going to Paris, via Madrid.
Has the tone of written language. ...をとおして is also used.

2. Nをつうじて

（1）その国くには一年いち ねんをつうじてあたたかい。／That country is warm all year round.
（2）このあたりは四季し きをつうじて観光客かん こう きゃくのたえることがない。／This region is constantly visited by tourists throughout the year.
（3）その作家さっ かは、生涯しょう がいを通つうじて、さまざまな形かたちで抑圧よく あつされてきた人々ひと びとを描えがきつづけた。／Throughout his life, that writer depicted people who had suffered various forms of oppression.
Attaches to nouns which demonstrate time, and means “throughout a fixed time period, without interruption.” Has the tone of written language. ...をとおして is also used.
