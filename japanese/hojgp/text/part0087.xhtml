【お...なさる】

［おR-なさる］

［ごNなさる］

（1）あの先生せん せいがお話はなしなさったことは、多おおくの人ひとたちにとって生いきていく心こころの支ささえとなるだろう。／What the teacher said will be a real comfort to many people in their lives.
（2）ケニアへはいつご出発しゅっ ぱつなさるんですか。／When are you going to depart for Kenya?
（3）今度こん どあなたがその方かた達たちとお食事しょく じなさるときにでも、一度いち どご一緒いっ しょさせていただけるとうれしいのですが。／I would be delighted if I could accompany you sometime when you have dinner with them again.
（4）どうぞ、お食たべなさい。／Please go ahead and eat. (Please help yourself.)
（5）明子あき こ、自己紹介じ こ しょう かいなさい。／Akiko, introduce yourself.
Takes either verb stems or nouns of Chinese origin that express an action. An honorific expression, like おR-になる. When used with a verb stem in expressions like お話しなさる ((someone of higher status) speaks) or お食べなさる ((someone of higher status) eats), it sounds a little old-fashioned. When used with a Chinese-origin noun, as in (2), it usually takes the form ごNなさる. When used with なさい (do something, imperative), as in (4) and (5), it is a polite imperative. However, in this case it is not used when talking to a person of higher status.
