【すえに】

［Nのすえに］

［V-たすえに］

（1）今月こん げつのすえに、首相しゅ しょうが訪中ほう ちゅうする。／The prime minister will visit China at the end of this month.
（2）長時間ちょう じ かんの協議きょう ぎのすえに、やっと結論けつ ろんが出でた。／After long hours of discussion, a conclusion was finally reached.
（3）かれは三年さん ねんの闘病とう びょう生活せい かつの末すえに亡なくなった。／He passed away after three years of struggle with the disease.
（4）よく考かんがえた末すえに決きめたことです。／It is a decision that was made after careful thought.
（5）大型おお がたトラックは1キロ暴走ぼう そうした末すえに、ようやく止とまった。／After driving out of control for a kilometer, the big truck finally came to a stop.
Means “at the end of a period of time.” In (1) it simply indicates the end of the time period, but it is often used with the meaning of “in the end, following a progression of events,” as shown in (2) and the rest of the examples. Tends to be used in written language.
