【ぱなし】

［R-っぱなし］

Attaches to the stem of a verb. Can also be used in the form R-はなし.

1. R-っぱなし〈non-interference〉

（1）ドアを開あけっ放ぱなしにしないでください。／Please don’t leave the door open.
（2）しまった。ストーブをつけっぱなしで出でてきてしまった。／Oh, no! I left home without switching the heater off.
（3）うちの子こときたら、食たべたら食たべっぱなし、服ふくは脱ぬいだら脱ぬぎっぱなしで、家いえの中なかがちっとも片かたづかない。／Our child leaves dirty dishes lying around after she eats and clothes lying around after she changes, so the house is never tidy.
Means that someone doesn’t do what s/he should do but “leaves something as it is,” or something “stays as it is” even though it shouldn’t. Unlike V-たまま, っぱなし often implies a negative evaluation of the action or event in question.

2. R-っぱなし〈duration〉

（1）新幹線しん かん せんはとても混こんでいて、東京とう きょうから大阪おお さかまで立たちっぱなしだった。／Because the shinkansen (bullet train) was very crowded, I had to stand all the way from Tokyo to Osaka.
（2）うちのチームはここの所ところずっと負まけっぱなしだ。／These days our team keeps on losing one game after another.
（3）今日きょうは失敗しっ ぱいばかりで、一いち日中にちじゅう文句もん くの言いわれっぱなしだった。／Today I did nothing but make mistakes, so I got told off all day long.
Means that the same state of affairs or the same situation continues for a long time.
