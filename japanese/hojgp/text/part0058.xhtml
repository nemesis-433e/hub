【いつしか】

（1）いつしかあたりは薄暗うす ぐらくなり、人影ひと かげもまばらになっていた。／Before I knew it, it had become dark and I could hardly see anybody.
（2）山やまもいつしか紅葉こう ように染そまり、秋あきが深ふかまっていた。／The next thing we knew, the trees on the mountains had turned color, and autumn was at its peak.
（3）いつしか雨あめも止やんで、空そらには虹にじがかかっていた。／The rain had stopped before I knew it, and a rainbow spanned the sky.
（4）去年きょ ねんまいた種たねがいつしか芽めを出だし、中なかにはつぼみをつけているものもあった。／The seeds I sowed last year had sprouted before I was aware of it, and some of them even had buds.
いつしか is an emphatic way to sayいつか and means “without realizing it, before one is aware.” It is written language and is often used in literary works.
