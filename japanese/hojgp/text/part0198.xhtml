【げ】

［Na げ］

［A-げ］

［R-げ］

（1）その人ひとは退屈たい くつげに雑誌ざっ しのページをめくっていた。／That person was leafing through the pages of a magazine in a bored manner.
（2）「そうですか」というその声こえには悲かなしげな響ひびきがあった。／The voice saying “Soo desuka (is that so?)” was tinged with sadness.
（3）彼女かの じょの笑顔え がおにはどこか寂さびしげなところがあった。／Her smiling face had something of a look of loneliness.
（4）彼かれのそのいわくありげな様子よう すが私わたしには気きになった。／There was something deliberate in his manner that bothered me.
げ is combined with the stem of an i-adjective or verb to create a na-adjective with the meaning “to have a certain look or manner.” The examples above can be paraphrased using ...そう, as in 退屈そう and 悲しそう, but ...げ is more for written language. There are also idiomatic expressions such as the one shown in (4).
