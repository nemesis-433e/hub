【てはならない】

［V-てはならない］

（1）一度いち どや二度に どの失敗しっ ぱいであきらめてはならない。／It doesn’t do to give up after only one or two failures.
（2）警察けい さつが来くるまで、だれもここに入はいってはならないそうだ。／Apparently no one is allowed to enter until the police arrive.
（3）ここで見みたり聞きいたりしたことは決けっして話はなしてはならないと言いわれた。／I’ve been told that I shouldn’t under any circumstances talk about anything I’ve seen or heard here.
Expresses prohibition. Often used to state general cautions or warnings, and only in special cases is it used to address someone directly regarding a particular incident. Used mostly in written language. Both V-てはならない and its more polite form V-てはなりません are used to address someone directly in special cases only. In spoken language, forms such as V-ちゃあだめだ or V-ちゃいけない are commonly used.
