【ともすると】

（1）ベテラン教師きょう しでもともするといい子こばかりに目めがいってしまう。／Even experienced teachers tend to pay attention only to the good pupils.
（2）この学生がく せいは時間じ かんにルーズで、ともすると授業じゅ ぎょうに1時間じ かんも平気へい きで遅おくれて来くる。／This student is careless about time, so she is apt to turn up for class as much as an hour late without thinking anything of it.
（3）夏なつはともすると睡眠すいみん不足ぶ そくになりがちである。／We tend to suffer from a lack of sleep in the summer.
（4）核兵器かく へい きはともすると人類じん るいの破滅は めつを引ひき起おこしかねない危険性き けん せいをはらんでいる。／Nuclear weapons are likely to harbor the danger of bringing about the destruction of mankind.
Expresses that a certain event is likely to happen as a result of some kind of triggering condition. Used most often with respect to the occurrence of undesirable situations and often appears with expressions such as ...がちだ (tend to...) and ...かねない (could...). ともすれば can be used instead of ともすると.
