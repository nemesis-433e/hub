【しいしい】

（1）女おんなは遠慮えん りょしいしい部屋へ やの片隅かた すみに座すわった。／The woman sat diffidently in a corner of the room.
（2）男おとこは大おおきなハンカチで汗あせをふきふき坂さかを登のぼってきた。／The man came up the slope, wiping at the sweat on his forehead with a large handkerchief.
（3）子供こ どもたちはもらったばかりのあめをなめなめ老人ろう じんについていった。／The children followed the old man as they licked away at the candy he had just given them.
A verb stem is repeated to express a repeated action, as in 食べ食べ (eat and eat) and 飲み飲み (drink and drink). For verbs such as する and みる, which have a verb stem of one syllable (i.e. し and み), い is added to create the forms しいしい and みいみい. Used when there is another concurrent action and takes the pattern しいしい...する. A somewhat old-fashioned idiomatic expression. In spoken language, ながら is used.
