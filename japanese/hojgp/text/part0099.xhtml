【おぼえはない】

1. V-られるおぼえはない

（1）きみにそんなひどいことを言いわれる覚おぼえはない。／I don’t remember doing anything that would make you say something so awful to me.
（2）おまえになぐられる覚おぼえはない。／I don’t remember doing anything to make you hit me.
（3）あなたのように冷つめたい人ひとに「冷淡れい たんだ」などと非難ひ なんされる覚おぼえはありません。／There is no reason why I should be called “heartless” by such a cold person as yourself.
Follows the passive form V-られる. Refers to an action of the person spoken to and means “I don’t remember doing anything that would make you take such an action.” A feeling of criticism is implied.

2. V-たおぼえはない

（1）彼かれがあんなに怒おこるようなことを言いった覚おぼえはないんだけど。／I really don’t remember saying anything to make him so angry.
（2）A:この間あいだの1万まん円えん、早はやく返かえしてもらえませんか。／About the 10,000 yen I lent you the other day... could you pay me back sometime soon?
B:何なんのことですか。私わたしはあなたにお金かねを借かりた覚おぼえはありませんが、他ほかの人ひとと間違ま ちがえているのではないですか。／What? I don’t remember borrowing money from you. Aren’t you confusing me with someone else?
（3）こちらは山田やま だにいじめられた覚おぼえはないのだが、山田やま だは「いじめて悪わるかった」と謝あやまってきた。／I don’t remember being bullied by Yamada, but Yamada apologized for bullying me.
Means the speaker has no recollection of an event or experience. Often used when the speaker is criticized and wants to justify him/herself.
