【てはだめだ】

1. V-てはだめだ〈prohibition〉

（1）駐車場ちゅう しゃ じょうで遊あそんではだめだ。出でて行いきなさい。／You can’t play in the parking lot. Please leave.
（2）「その花はなをとってはだめよ。」と姉あねが弟おとうとに言いった。／“You mustn’t pick that flower,” she told her brother.
（3）文句もん くばかり言いっていてはだめだ。自分じ ぶんでなんとかしろ。／Don’t just complain. Do something on your own.
（4）こんなところでへばってはだめだ。あと1キロだ。しっかりしろ。／You can’t collapse at this point. There’s just one more kilometer to go. Pull yourself together!
（5）「今いま、あの人ひとを叱しかってはだめです。もうすこし様子よう すを見みましょう。」と彼女かの じょは部下ぶ かをかばった。／“It won’t do to reprimand him now. Let’s wait a little longer and see what happens,” she said, coming to the defense of her subordinate.
（6）そんな浅あさいところから、飛とび込こんではだめだ。／You must not dive from such a shallow place.
Expresses prohibition. Often used by people in a supervisory position, such as teachers, parents, and managers, when they are speaking to someone being supervised. Often reduced from では to じゃ, or from ては to ちゃ. Same as V-てはいけない.

2. ...てはだめだ

［N／Na　ではだめだ］

［A-くてはだめだ］

［V-ていてはだめだ］

（1）写真しゃ しんをとるのに、こんなに暗くらくてはだめだ。／It’s too dark here for taking photos.
（2）秘書ひ しょになりたいそうだが、言葉こと ばがそんなにぞんざいではだめだ。／She says she wants to be a secretary, but her rough way of speaking is a real problem.
（3）富士山ふ じ さんへ登のぼりたいのなら、そんな靴くつではだめです。登山靴と ざん ぐつをはきなさい。／If you want to climb Fuji-san (Mt. Fuji), those shoes are no good. Wear climbing boots.
（4）今いまでもお母かあさんに洗濯せん たくしてもらっているんですか。それではだめです。自立じ りつしたかったら、自分じ ぶんでやりなさい。／Are you still having your mother do your wash? That’s no good! If you want to be independent, do it yourself.
（5）君きみのように遊あそんでばかりいてはだめだ。学生がく せいは勉強べん きょうしなくてはいけない。／You can’t just fool around and have fun all the time. Students have to study!
Expresses the judgment that under a certain condition, it will be impossible to attain a certain goal. The goal is sometimes expressed in words, and sometimes only alluded to in the context. Often used in conversation. The contracted forms じゃ（あ）だめだ and ちゃ（あ）だめだ are also used.
As in (5), this form may also be combined with verbs to create the construction V-ていてはだめだ, which is used to state that the present situation is unacceptable and to criticize or reprimand someone.
