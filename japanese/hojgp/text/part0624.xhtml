【ども1】

［Nども］

（1）申もうし訳わけありません。私わたしどもの責任せき にんです。／I am very sorry. This is our fault.
（2）手前て まえどもの店みせでは、この品物しな ものは扱あつかっておりません。／I’m afraid that we don’t carry this product.
（3）《けんかのことば》野郎や ろうども、みんなそろってかかって来こい。／《fighting words》Hey, you bunch of jerks, bring it on!
（4）政界せい かいは偽善者ぎ ぜん しゃどもの集あつまりだ。／Political circles are gatherings of hypocrites.
Indicates a plural number and attaches mainly to nouns that refer to people. Similar to たち. However, when it attaches to a first-person pronoun, it is more polite than 私たち because it conveys a humble attitude. Another difference is that 私たち may or may not include the listener, but 私ども always excludes the listener. As shown in (3) and (4), when ども is used with a second- or third-person pronoun, it usually conveys a sense of contempt towards that person. Other examples include 女ども (the women), 者ども (all of you), and so on.
