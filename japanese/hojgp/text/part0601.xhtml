【とても】

1. とても

（1）あの映画えい がはとても面白おも しろかった。／That movie was really interesting!
（2）今度こん どの新入社員しん にゅう しゃ いんはとてもよく働はたらく。／The new employee works very hard.
Expresses an extreme degree of something. Very, remarkably, extremely.

2. とても...ない

（1）こんな難むずかしい問題もん だいはとても私わたしには解とけません。／A problem this difficult is quite impossible for me to solve.
（2）一度いち どにこんなにたくさんの単語たん ごはとても覚おぼえられません。／There’s absolutely no way I can memorize so many words all at once.
（3）あの美うつくしさはとても言葉こと ばでは表現ひょう げんできない。／That kind of beauty really cannot be expressed in words.
Expresses a subjective judgment by the speaker that something is futile or impossible, no matter what methods or means s/he might try to use. In written language, とても...ない can be replaced by とうてい...ない.
