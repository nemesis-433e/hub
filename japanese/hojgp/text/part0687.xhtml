【なぜか】

（1）最近さい きんなぜか家族か ぞくのことが気きにかかってしかたがない。／I don’t know why, but my family has really been on my mind recently.
（2）彼かれは今日きょうはなぜか元気げん きがないようだ。／Somehow he just doesn’t seem like himself today.
（3）だめだと思おもってたのに、なぜか希望き ぼうしていた会社かい しゃに採用さい ようされてしまった。／Even though I thought it was hopeless, for some reason I got hired by the company of my choice.
Expresses the feeling “I don’t know the cause or reason, but....” Often used when the speaker is describing something that contradicts his/her senses, intentions, or expectations.
